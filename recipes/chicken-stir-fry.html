Chicken Stir Fry

Tender strips of chicken sizzle in a wok, dancing with an array of colorful vegetables, kissed by aromatic spices and savory sauces, creating a symphony of flavors and textures in every bite.

Steps

  1. Marinate the thinly sliced chicken in soy sauce for about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  3. Add minced garlic and ginger to the hot oil and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the wok and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until cooked through and golden brown. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.
  5. Add assorted vegetables to the wok and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. You can start with the vegetables that take longer to cook and add quicker-cooking ones later.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the wok with the vegetables and toss everything together.
  7. Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add extra soy sauce or other sauces for more flavor if desired.
  8. Transfer the stir-fry chicken and vegetables to a serving dish and serve hot, optionally garnished with chopped green onions or sesame seeds.

Indulge in the enticing flavors and textures of this quick and wholesome stir-fry chicken, a delightful dish that's sure to tantalize your taste buds.

### kcal

1h 15 min

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh, thinly sliced
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, minced
  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: onions, mushrooms, chili peppers for added flavor and heat
