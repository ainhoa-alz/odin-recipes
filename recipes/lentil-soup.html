Lentil Soup

A comforting vegetarian lentil soup, rich in earthy and aromatic flavors, simmered slowly with fresh vegetables and fragrant spices, offering a nourishing delight in every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams dry lentils (or 2 cans, drained and rinsed)
  • 1.5 liters vegetable broth or water
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced (or 1 can, diced)
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 5 ml paprika
  • 5 ml cumin
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. If using dry lentils, rinse them under cold water and drain. Set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, celery, and bell pepper. Saut√© for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Add the minced garlic, paprika, cumin, and bay leaves to the pot. Stir well and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and lentils to the pot. Stir to combine with the vegetables and spices.
  5. Pour in the vegetable broth or water, ensuring that the lentils and vegetables are submerged. Bring the soup to a boil.
  6. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

This soup pairs well with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal. Buon appetito!
